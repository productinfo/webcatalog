How It Works

WebCatalog lets you turn any web apps into native-like desktop apps (also called site-specific browsers). Now, with WebCatalog, you can launch your favorite apps quickly in self-contained, distraction-free windows. Each app's data is stored separately, protecting you from cross-website trackers and preserving your privacy.
